SARL Community

Resources

Got Questions?

The SARL forum is the first source for getting answers in case you got stuck. The community is very friendly. The questions are special issues on Github.com that are labeled with "question". Please ask any questions clearly and with sufficient information attached.

Found a Bug?

Bug reports and enhancement requests are tracked at GitHub.com. Please explain the problem and provide a reduced but reproducable example. Also please explain the concrete use case when requesting enhancements.

Professional Support

Need training, problem solving, a prototype, or just a SARL-based product? Multiagent Group (University of Technology of Belfort-Montbéliard, France) and GITIA (Facultad Regional Tucumán, Universidad Tecnológica Nacional, Argentina) offer all kinds of professional consulting around SARL.
